## FAQ

**How long does each course take?**
Depends on your pace. AI Fluency: a few days. Product Thinking and Level Up AI: a few weeks. GitHub course: ~2.5 hours.

**Do I get a certificate?**
No. The value is in the skills, not the paper.

**Can I skip courses?**
Yes. Each course is self-contained. The tracks are recommended progressions, not requirements.

**Why is this free?**
AI tools cost money. AI knowledge shouldn't. Built by a practitioner who believes AI literacy is not a luxury good.
